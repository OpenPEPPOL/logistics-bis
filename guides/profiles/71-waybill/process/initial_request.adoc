= Initial Waybill

The following diagram shows a process where the Consignor or a party handling on behalf of the Consignor sends a Waybill to a Carrier.

image::images/initial.png[image,width=945,height=1064]

Parties involved:

*Consignor:* The party preparing and sending the Waybill describing the transport service.

*Freight Forwarder:* The party preparing and sending the Waybill on behalf of the Consignor.

*Carrier:* The party providing the transport service as described in the Waybill.
